Welcome to our regional overview page. Below, you will find information detailing our designated technological centers. These hubs serve as incubators for innovation, fostering collaboration between private enterprise, academic institutions, and local communities.

Future Expansion Plans

As the maps illustrate, our current footprint is strategically distributed to maximize resource allocation. Moving forward, the development committee is evaluating secondary markets for subsequent rollouts. Criteria for selection will include proximity to universities, existing infrastructure, and community support initiatives.

If you have questions about specific locations or wish to submit a proposal for a future hub, please reach out to our community engagement team.
